    Nuggets are a collection of quotes or moments taken from '27 students that are seen to be "controversial" and false. Almost all nuggets are quotes about the person who said it, and are defensive statements that have been determined to be incorrect. The term "nugget" comes from an assignment from an AP Government assignment where students were instrucuted to find 3 pieces of evidence, labeled Nugget 1 through 3.
